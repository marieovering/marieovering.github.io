Washington DC Altitude Map Graphic

An ArcGIS Story Map

This DC altitude Map Graphic was made using ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator. The Contours were processed and symbology was defined in Arcgis Pro. The DC Boundary and Rivers were added as well. Lastly, in Adobe Illustrator bridges were added and the design was finalized.
